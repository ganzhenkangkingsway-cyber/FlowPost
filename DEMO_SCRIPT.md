# 3-Minute Live Demo Script

**Total Duration: 3 minutes**

---

## Introduction (15 seconds)

"Hi everyone! Today I'm excited to show you our social media management platform that uses AI to help you create, schedule, and manage posts across multiple platforms. Let's dive right in."

---

## 1. Dashboard Overview (20 seconds)

**Action:** Show the main dashboard
- "Here's your command center. At a glance, you can see your upcoming posts, recent activity, and connected social media accounts."
- "The clean interface makes it easy to navigate between creating posts, viewing your calendar, and analyzing performance."

---

## 2. AI Caption Generator (45 seconds)

**Action:** Navigate to AI Caption Generator
- "One of our most powerful features is the AI Caption Generator."
- **Upload an image** (use a prepared professional image)
- **Click "AI Generate"**
- "Watch as our AI analyzes the image and generates three unique, engaging captions in real-time."
- **Wait for captions to appear**
- "Each caption is tailored with relevant hashtags and emojis, optimized for social media engagement."
- **Click "Generate More Variations"**
- "Need more options? Generate completely different captions with just one click. Each generation uses different tones - inspirational, casual, professional, or enthusiastic."

---

## 3. Create Post with Platform Previews (40 seconds)

**Action:** Navigate to Create Post
- "Now let's create an actual post."
- **Upload an image or use the design tools**
- "You can upload images, videos, or even design graphics right here with our built-in Pixlr integration."
- **Paste one of the AI-generated captions**
- "Here's where it gets interesting - see these live previews? This is exactly how your post will look on Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok, and X."
- **Show the different platform previews**
- "No more guessing - you see the final result before publishing."

---

## 4. Calendar View (30 seconds)

**Action:** Navigate to Calendar View
- "Let's look at the content calendar."
- **Show the calendar with scheduled posts**
- "Here you can see all your scheduled posts at a glance. Each post shows which platforms it's going to."
- **Click on a post to show the preview modal**
- "Click any post to see full details, preview it, or make quick edits."
- "You can filter by platform to focus on specific channels."

---

## 5. Team Collaboration (20 seconds)

**Action:** Navigate to Team page
- "Collaboration is built in. Invite team members, assign roles, and manage permissions."
- "Your marketing team can work together seamlessly - designers, copywriters, and managers all in one place."

---

## 6. Analytics (15 seconds)

**Action:** Navigate to Analytics
- "Track your performance with detailed analytics."
- "See engagement metrics, reach, and performance across all platforms to optimize your content strategy."

---

## Closing (15 seconds)

**Action:** Return to dashboard
- "And that's it! From AI-powered caption generation to multi-platform scheduling and analytics - everything you need to elevate your social media presence in one platform."
- "Thanks for watching! Try it yourself and see how much time you can save."

---

## Tips for Demo Success

### Before the Demo:
1. **Prepare assets:** Have 2-3 high-quality images ready to upload
2. **Create sample posts:** Schedule 3-4 posts on the calendar for visual impact
3. **Test the AI:** Ensure Perplexity API is working (or fallback is enabled)
4. **Connect accounts:** Have at least 2-3 social platforms connected
5. **Clear browser cache:** Ensure smooth performance

### During the Demo:
- **Speak confidently and at a steady pace**
- **Keep mouse movements smooth and deliberate**
- **If AI generation takes time:** Fill the gap by talking about the technology
- **Have a backup image** in case of upload issues
- **Practice transitions** between sections

### Pro Tips:
- Use keyboard shortcuts where possible for smoother navigation
- Zoom browser to 110-125% for better visibility on screen recordings
- If doing live, have the script on a second monitor
- Pause briefly between sections to let features sink in
- Smile and maintain energy throughout!
